About Us:

Dr. D. Y. Patil Public School was established way back in 1992 in the form of seed with 120 children and 3 teachers. The school is today a deep-rooted and fruit-laden tree with more than 1300 children and a good number of teachers.
The school provides a free and healthy atmosphere to inculcate knowledge, sense of responsibility towards one's family, school and Nation. The school is affiliated to CBSE Board. The medium of instruction at school is English. The school has classes from playgroup to class 10.

Admission Policies

*Please note that Admissions at Dr. D. Y. Patil Public School, Pimpri, Pune are on a first come first serve basis and seats are limited.
Admissions will only be confirmed once the necessary documents are submitted in the school
office and online payment of admission fees and first term fees have been done.


The following documents are required to be submitted:

    • Photo copy of Progress Report (Previous Class).
    • Original copy of the previous School Leaving Certificate/Transfer Certificate
    • Photo copy of Birth Certificate
    • 2 Passport size photographs.
    • Caste certificate if applicable
    • Medical fitness certificate
    • UDISE Number (Unified District information System for Education) from current school (applicable for admissions from Class 2 onwards)
    • Completely filled Admission form.
    • Income Proof
